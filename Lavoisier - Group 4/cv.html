Objective

The main of objective for thr creation of this website is to guide you through Chapters 18-22 in the book known as the "Noli Me Tangere" or the "Touch Me Not" in English. In this website we will show you what can be seen in chapters 18-22, the people involved in this chapter, and our own personal reflection about it. We hope that this can help you a lot so that you can understand these chapters.

Reasons

Education

- One reason for the creation of this website is for us to learn the diffrent steps into creating a website. This also helps us learn the Noli Me Tangere better.

Teamwork

- Another reason is for us to learn better cooperation. If a man can't handle a task alone, then they shall need allies to fix it. A group can't be called a group if even the smallest member has none t to contribute.

Time Management

- Given a desired time limit, we are forced to do such a task to do. This can help us learn to manage our time especially that we know that it would affect other people if we don't cooperate.
